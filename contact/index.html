    How to get Ryu Clinic

    Address 203A - 130 Brew St. Port Moody, BC, V3H 0E3
    Phone 604.469.2000
    Email info@ryu.clinic

    By Driving

    We have 90 min free underground parking. All vehicles are required to be registered when you park1. There is free underground parking off Morrissey Road. It’s all connected to Thrifty food store parkade. You can come up directly from P1(Liquor store parking area) with elevator. Directions

    By Skytrain

    Evergreen line to Inlet Centre Station. And It takes 4 min walking to the clinic. Directions
